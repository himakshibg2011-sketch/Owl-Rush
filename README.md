INTRO

In this web browser game, you will fly as an owl and will have to dodge the upcoming pipes to survive. the moment you touch the any of the pipe, you will die. Play and compete with your friends and see who lasts the longest!

HOW DOES THE GAME WORK

In this web playable game, you will first land on a homepage in which you will see thre buttons:

1.Mode

2.Music

3.Play

First choose the mode you would like to play (easy or hard) then, on/off the music as per your will by clicking on the music button and then click the play button to start the game.

HOW TO PLAY

After starting the game, you will have to dodge the upcoming pipes in order to survive the game, you will be playing as an owl and in order to dodge the pipes you will have to fly through space between the pipes. Compete with your friends and check who gets the highest score through the end screen.
